Suicidal thoughts
in the United States

This data shows the percentage of adults (ages 18 and older) who thought about commiting suicide in the past year. The respondents were given a questionare. They were asked, "At any time in the past 12 months, did you seriously think about trying to kill yourself? Those who answered yes are shown in this data set. The data was collected between 2012 -2014.
